What Is Pomodoro?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that involves breaking work into intervals called Pomodoros, followed by short breaks.

Steps:

  1. Choose a task to work on.
  2. Set a timer for 25 minutes (one Pomodoro).
  3. Work on the task until the timer rings.
  4. Take a 5-minute break after each Pomodoro.
  5. After four Pomodoros, take a longer break (15-30 minutes).

Benefits:

  • Improves focus and productivity.
  • Prevents burnout with regular breaks.
  • Helps maintain consistent work intervals.
